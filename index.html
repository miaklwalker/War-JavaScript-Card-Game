War

Rules

The game of war is a simple card game where the cards of a deck are evenly divided among two players.
The player then take turns drawing cards and the person that draws the card worth the most that round wins.
If they players draw the same card war begins each player places three cards face down.
Then draw one more and this card decides who gets all cards on the table.
